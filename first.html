History of Camry

Ceilca Camry

The name "Camry" started on the four door sedan which looked similar to the Toyta Celica calling it Celica Camry. Celica Camry was first released to the public in 1980s. If you thought that was the Celica was the first generation of Camry, you are no fan, it was just its predecessor. The actual car is the Carina. By modifying the front end and adding some cues. The time period was late 1970s and early 1980s.
